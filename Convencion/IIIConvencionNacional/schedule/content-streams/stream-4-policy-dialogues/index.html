Stream 4: Policy dialogues

This stream pulls together work developed by a number of leading organisations and will look into how an effective policy framework can contribute to more sustainable management of our natural capital.

During sessions within this stream, we will cover the wide range of policy options - from regulatory to voluntary - exploring collaborations between business, finance and government.

Topics within this stream will include:

  • Disaster risk reduction
  • Mapping the policy landscape
  • Rediscovering the pre-competitive space and its role in a healthy market system
