D3: Workshop

Nature and the art of policy making

The Natural Capital Protocol is an important step on the way to helping businesses integrate natural capital into mainstream decision-making. Yet, both natural and societal systems are highly complex and interconnected, which may warrant framing natural capital through a more systemic lens. In turn this may suggest complementary policy approaches. With this session we report on our findings and invite those interested to join a systems conversation. We reflect on the current ‘theory of change’, including what assumptions this may hold on the way the relevant businesses are interconnected with each other, as well as with economic and societal systems
