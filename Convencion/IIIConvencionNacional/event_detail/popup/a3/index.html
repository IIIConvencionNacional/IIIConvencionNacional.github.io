A3: Quick-fire case studies

Putting familiar tools to better use

Businesses already have a host of tools they are using on a daily basis which could potentially be modified to help manage natural capital opportunities and risks more effectively. This ‘quick fire’ session will look at using existing business management tools, such as risk registers and balance sheets, to help new adopters rapidly get to grips with natural capital.

Room: Fintry Auditorium

Chaired by:

Jane Gleeson-White, author of 'Six Capitals: The revolution capitalism has to have – or can accountants save the planet?'

 

Speakers include:
