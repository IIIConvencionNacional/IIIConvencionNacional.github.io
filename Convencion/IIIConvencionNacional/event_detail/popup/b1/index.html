B1: Panel Q&A

How can an understanding of natural capital help to drive innovation and better decision making?

 

Measuring and valuing our impacts and dependencies on natural capital can bring benefits far beyond the mitigation of environmental risks. It can increase opportunities and improve strategic management and decision making. This group of experienced practitioners will share valuable lessons from their innovative approaches and also share their explorations into the realm of social capital valuation.
