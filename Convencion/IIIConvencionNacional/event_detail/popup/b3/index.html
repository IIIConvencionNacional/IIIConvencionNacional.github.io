B3: Workshop

Finding your way through the maze

 

Are there (too) many natural capital tools already available? This session will highlight the wealth of tools available, and discuss how to help users find their way through this maze. Through an interactive on-line polling system as well as breakout table discussions, we will explore what categories of tools exist, whether the multitude of different tools is a problem, and why / why not. Participants will discuss which solutions could increase uptake towards better informed natural capital assessments. Could a future Toolkit to accompany the upcoming Natural Capital Protocol be beneficial?
