D6: Panel discussion

Final session in the Policy Dialogues stream, bringing together the topics that have emerged over the past two days. 

 

Expert panellists will share their perspectives on the latest developments in the natural capital world from the policy makers point of view.The session will draw together the main themes that people are actually talking about in relation to natural capital at different scales. They will also comment on what they think needs to happen next to drive change, in our cities, municipalities and countries. 

 

Room: Tinto

Chair:

Richard Spencer, Head of Sustainability, Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales

Speakers include:

Frank Hawkins, Director, IUCN Washington

Michelle Lapinski, President, Sustain Biz

Steven Bullock, Global Head of Research, Trucost
