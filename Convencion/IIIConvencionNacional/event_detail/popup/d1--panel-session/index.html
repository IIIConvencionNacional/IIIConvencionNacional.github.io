D1: Panel discussion

Re-thinking the importance of natural capital  

Pressures on natural capital are ever increasing, exacerbated by both climate change and ecosystem degradation. This panel session will explore natural capital in the context of risk reduction, at different scales, by considering the risks associated with mismanagement of natural capital and the economic, social and ecological opportunities to be gained from more intelligent management and restoration of ecosystems at different scales.We will look at what we can learn from the full range of risks associated with mismanagement of natural capital and what government and policy-makers, are doing to address this.
