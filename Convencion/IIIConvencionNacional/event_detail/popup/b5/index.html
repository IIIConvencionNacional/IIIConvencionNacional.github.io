B5: Case studies

A close look at two companies with consumer-facing brands that are using natural capital-based tools, strategies and management. This session will examine the importance of public opinion, both local to production and among consumers more widely. The session will also examine how a global overview can guide local scale actions to promote sustainable use of natural capital. 

 

Room: Sidlaw Auditorium

Chaired by: 
Eva Zabey, Director: Redefining Value, Natural Capital, WBCSD 

Speakers include:

Antje Ahrends, Head of Genetics and Conservation, Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh

Carina Mueller,  Environmental Sustainability Scientist, Unilever

Michael Alexander, Head of Environment, Water, Agriculture Sustainability, Diageo
