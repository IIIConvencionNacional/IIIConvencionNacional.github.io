A6: Case studies

Case studies: real-life stories illustrating the journey from risk to opportunity

 

Several leading organisations have already demonstrated how they have turned natural capital from a risk to an opportunity. In this session, delegates will hear two in-depth case studies which will explain the practical steps required to move from ‘risk to opportunity’ and the benefits of doing so.
 

Room: Moorfoot

Gerard Bos, Director: Global Business and Biodiversity Programme, IUCN
