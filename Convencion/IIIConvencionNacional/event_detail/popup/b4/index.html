B4: Quick-fire case studies

Question and learn from organisations that have worked collaboratively to innovate in the natural capital arena

This session will give delegates a chance to question and learn from companies that have worked collaboratively to innovate in the natural capital arena. 

Dr George Cobb and Nicola Melville will jointly present the work of the VALUES Project which developed a prototype spatial tool to help with route selection of linear infrastructure. The tool determines which ecosystems and services are potentially affected, and their sensitivity to the development.

Monique van den Dungen of Groningen Seaports will present their innovative work on large-scale sludge management which they carried out in partnership with various agricultural, NGO and government stakeholders. 

Rudi Daelmans of Desso Group will present Desso's work on product innovation and supply chain in association with various partners and stakeholders. 

 

Room: Sidlaw Auditorium

Session chair:

Will Evison, Assistant Director and Environmental Economist, PriceWaterhouseCoopers

Speakers include:

Dr. George Cobb, Group Sustainability Accountant, Scottish and Southern Energy 

Nicola Melville, Senior Specialist Scientist, Scottish Environment Protection Agency

Rudi Daelmans, Sustainability Director, Desso Group

Monique van den Dungen, Manager: Natural environment, Groningen Seaports
