C5: Workshop

What do shareholders need to know in relation to natural capital? What should you be reporting and what natural capital indicators should you be looking at? 

What are shareholders starting to expect?

There are a large number of stakeholders in the natural capital space – government, civil society, corporations, finance and individual shareholders. This workshop will focus on material natural capital information and disclosure; the session will take the form of an interactive workshop with participants at roundtables. The aim is to gather the various stakeholders to discuss the potential diverging and converging interests and needs of these groups, specifically around the topic of disclosure and the changing expectations of shareholders. It will feature a high-level panel but all participants are expected to contribute in the roundtables by tackling questions head on. Input on these questions will be sought from the tables and then discussed by the panel.
