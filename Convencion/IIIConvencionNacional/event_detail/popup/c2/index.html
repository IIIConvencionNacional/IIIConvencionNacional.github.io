C2: Case studies

Natural capital and the financial sector: an evidence-based approach to change

Case studies looking at the importance of taking an evidence-based approach to decision-making. We will look at how this can be translated into metrics that enable business leaders to tackle new challenges.

Neil Brown and Emma Lupton will present Alliance Trusts work on integrating water into our investment decisions. This case study will highlights how companies can manage water to outperform, how investors can identify those that will, how to integrate water into financial statements and therefore, into investor decision making. 

Yiannis Bartzilas will present  the Water Risk Valuation Tool (developed by Bloomberg LP and the Natural Capital Declaration. The WRVT is a practical, high-level demonstration tool that illustrates how water risk can be incorporated into company valuation in the mining sector using familiar financial modelling techniques.
