Neil Brown

Investment Manager, pan-European Equities
Alliance Trust

Neil has worked in investment for 13 years. Before joining Alliance Trust Investments in August 2012, Neil spent four years at Aviva Investors where, most recently, he was an SRI Fund Manager.

Neil’s investment career started at Pensions & Investments Research Consultants where he was a senior researcher. In 2004, he moved to Threadneedle Asset Management where, as well as having the role of Pan European Equity Analyst, he was also Head of Governance and Responsible Investment.

Neil is lead fund manager on the SF European Growth OEIC and SF Pan European SICAV. He is co-fund manager on the SF UK Growth and UK Ethical funds. He has an MSc in Development Economics from the School of African & Oriental Studies and a BA (Hons) in Economics from the University of Manchester. He holds the CFA Society of the UK Investment Management Certificate.  

Neil is a member of the Listed Equity Steering Committee of the United Nations supported Principles for Responsible Investment. He is also the immediate past Chair of the UNPRI Integration Working Group.
