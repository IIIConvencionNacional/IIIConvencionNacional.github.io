Daan Wensing

Managing Director, Leaders for Nature

Daan Wensing is managing director of the Leaders for Nature network and head of all IUCN NL’s business and biodiversity programmes. This includes the international expansion of Leaders for Nature, to India and other countries.
Before joining IUCN NL, Daan co-founded the company Triple E and was the director of one of it’s business units. He developed business models for biodiversity and ecosystems in the Netherlands and abroad (including many EU countries, India, USA, Brazil). Mr. Wensing has a MSc in biology from Utrecht University and a Masters in Science and Business from Utrecht University in cooperation with the Netherlands Business University Nyenrode.
