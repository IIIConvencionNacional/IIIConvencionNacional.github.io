Professor Dieter Helm

Chair
Natural Capital Committee
 

Dieter is an Official Fellow in Economics at New College, Oxford, Professor of Energy Policy at the University of Oxford and Professorial Research Fellow of the Smith School of Enterprise and the Environment. He was Chair of the Natural Capital Committee (2012-2015) and his most recent book, Natural Capital - Valuing The Planet, was published in May 2015 by Yale University Press.
