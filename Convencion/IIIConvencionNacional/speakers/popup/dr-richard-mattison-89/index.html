Dr Richard Mattison

Chief Executive
Trucost

Richard is Chief Executive of Trucost Plc, the leading provider of natural capital measurement and valuation. Richard has advised various UN bodies and governments on environmental reporting and ecosystem services and has led groundbreaking projects including developing the world’s first Environmental Profit and Loss account for PUMA.
 

Richard is also a Member of the World Economic Forum Global Agenda Council on Biodiversity & Natural Capital, member of the Oxford University Smith School Stranded Asset Programme consultative panel, and advisor to The Economics of Ecosystems and Biodiversity (TEEB) coalition.
