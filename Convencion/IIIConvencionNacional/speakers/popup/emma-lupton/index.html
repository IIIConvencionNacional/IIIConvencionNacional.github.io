Emma Lupton

European Water Account Manager
CDP

Emma is the European Water Account manager at CDP focused on driving corporate water stewardship in Europe.

Prior to CDP Emma worked with Alliance Trust to formalise the integration of water into their decision making process. Previously Emma managed the corporate water stewardship programme for Global Water Intelligence and she has a background in wealth management. She holds an MSc from Imperial College London in Environmental Technology, specialising in Water Management and the Investment Management Certificate of the CFA Society of the UK.
