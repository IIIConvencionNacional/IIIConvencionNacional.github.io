Rachel Depree

Senior Manager, Engagement
Sky

Rachel Depree works for Sky, one of Europe’s leading entertainment companies. She is part of Sky’s sustainability team, called seeing the bigger picture where she leads stakeholder engagement strategy. She is now managing Sky’s partnership with WWF, building on the successful work carried out over the last six years on Sky Rainforest Rescue.

A New Zealander, Rachel has worked in the sustainability sector in business and in government for over fifteen years and was formerly a General Manager at the Ministry for the Environment in New Zealand, working closely with business on climate change and waste policy.
