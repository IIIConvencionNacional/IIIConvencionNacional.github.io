Shaun Kingsbury

CEO, UK Green Investment Bank

Shaun Kingsbury has been CEO of the UK Green Investment Bank since October 2012. Previously he served as an Investment Partner at Hudson Clean Energy Partners, a leading clean energy private equity firm where he was responsible for its European activities. He is a long time renewable energy investor and advisor having been a founding partner of Pulsar Energy Capital and an advisor to 3i on a number of renewable energy transaction opportunities in Europe.
