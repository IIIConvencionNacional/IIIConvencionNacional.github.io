Klas Hallberg

Manager New Developments in Sustainability
AkzoNobel

Klas Hallberg is Manager of New Developments at AkzoNobel, since starting at the company in 2006.

Akzonobel is the largest global paints and coatings company and a major producer of specialty chemicals. Headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, it is has operations in more than 80 countries and 47 000 employees around the world. AkzoNobel 2014 revenue was 14 300 M€.

Klas has a  Masters degree in Chemical Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg, Sweden.
