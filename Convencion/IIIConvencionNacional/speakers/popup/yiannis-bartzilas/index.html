Yiannis Bartzilas

Environmental, Social and Governance Analyst
Bloomberg

Yiannis is ESG Analyst at Bloomberg. He is responsible for the acquisition, maintenance and quality control of Bloomberg's ESG data as well as the development and maintenance of respective ESG Tools and research provided on the Terminal.

Furthermore, Yiannis liaises and supports Bloomberg users with the integration of ESG data in their analysis. He brings further experience in the sustainability sector and more specifically in the carbon market, where he worked as Portfolio Manager at First Climate. There, he procured carbon certificates, performed due diligence and evaluated economic impacts of carbon offset projects around the world.

Yiannis holds an MSc in Ecological Economics from the University of Edinburgh, UK
