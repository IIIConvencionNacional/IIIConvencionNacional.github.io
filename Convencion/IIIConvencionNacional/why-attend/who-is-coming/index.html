Who's coming

The 2015 World Forum on Natural Capital will bring together business leaders, government representatives and environmental experts from around the globe. The event will examine the most up-to-date developments in this rapidly evolving field, with a focus on managing risk and driving innovation.

Below is a snapshot of some of the organisations that are coming to Edinburgh this November:

  • Credit Suisse
  • Mars Inc.
  • Skanska
  • Tetra Pak
